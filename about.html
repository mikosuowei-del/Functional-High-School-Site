About Geniuses Model Academy

Committed to developing exceptional thinkers and leaders.

Founded as a center for gifted education, Geniuses Model Academy combines a challenging curriculum with comprehensive student support. We emphasize project-based learning, interdisciplinary study, and mentorship programs that connect students with industry and research partners.

Accreditations, awards, and partnerships support our mission and ensure continuous improvement across academics and student services.
