Admissions

Join Geniuses Model Academy — where potential meets opportunity.

We admit students based on academic readiness, intellectual curiosity, and fit with our community. Application steps include an online application, prior transcripts, recommendation letters, and either an entrance assessment or portfolio review depending on program.

Key dates: Early decision — November 1, Regular — January 15.
